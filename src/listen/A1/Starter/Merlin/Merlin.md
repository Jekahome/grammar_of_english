<summary>Chapter 1</summary>

<div class="book-page">
  <div class="chapter-label">Chapter 1</div>
  <div class="chapter-title">The Boy Merlin</div>
  <div class="chapter-divider"><span> ~ ~ ~ </span></div>
  <div class="book-body">
    <p class="first-para">Young Merlin lives in a castle with his mother, Princess Adhan. The castle is in Wales, in the west of King Vortigern's kingdom. Merlin doesn't know his father. He's fighting for the king far away.</p>

<p>The castle is near a forest. Merlin and his mother often walk there. She teaches him about forest plants.</p>

<p>"When you're older, you can come here without me," she says, "but be careful. There are many hunters and an enchantress, Keridwen, lives here too."</p>

<p>Merlin's only friend is a toad.</p>

<p>One day Merlin visits the village near the castle. Two boys, Morfran and Arwell, are behind him, but Merlin doesn't see them. He talks to his toad.</p>

<p>"Merlin's talking to his toad!" Morfran laughs.</p>

<p>"Go away," Merlin says, "or when my father comes back home..."</p>

<p>"We're not afraid, Merlin. Your father's never at home," Arwell says. "And is he truly your father? Perhaps you're a demon's child."</p>

<p>That night, Merlin tells his mother everything. "What can I do about these boys?" he asks.</p>

<p>"I can talk to those bullies," Princess Adhan says.</p>

<p>"No," Merlin answers. "They call me a mother's boy without that."</p>

<p>"Then your father can speak to them after he comes home." She smiles. "But be careful with Morfran. He's Keridwen's son."</p>

<summary>Chapter 2</summary>

<div class="book-page">
  <div class="chapter-label">Chapter 2</div>
  <div class="chapter-title">The Bullies Come Back</div>
  <div class="chapter-divider"><span> ~ ~ ~ </span></div>
  <div class="book-body">
    <p class="first-para">Next week, Merlin goes to the village shop with his mother. He sits near the door with his toad in his hand. Suddenly, Morfran and Arwell jump out in front of him.</p>

<p>"It's Merlin and his toad!" they cry.</p>

<p>The toad is afraid. It falls to the ground.</p>

<p>"Let's see your toad jump!" Morfran laughs. He runs after it.</p>

<p>When Merlin's mother arrives, the bullies leave.</p>

<p>That night, Merlin sees his father in a dream. He is sitting under an oak tree after a fight with the king's enemies.</p>

<p>"I'm dying," Merlin's father tells him.</p>

<p>"Father, no. I need your help with some bullies!" Merlin cries.</p>

<p>"When they bully you, walk away," his father says. "Look for the biggest oak in the forest. There's something important near it."</p>

<p>Next morning, Merlin tells his mother about the dream.</p>

<p>"Listen to your father," she says quietly.</p>

<p>When Merlin meets the bullies again, they take his toad.</p>

<p>"Say goodbye to Mr Toad!" Morfran laughs.</p>

<p>Merlin remembers his dream and walks into the forest.</p>

<summary>Chapter 3</summary>

<div class="book-page">
  <div class="chapter-label">Chapter 3</div>
  <div class="chapter-title">The Enchantress</div>
  <div class="chapter-divider"><span> ~ ~ ~ </span></div>
  <div class="book-body">
    <p class="first-para">Merlin lives in the forest for days and eats forest plants. Then one day he finds the biggest oak in the forest. There's a little house near it. In front of the house, there's a boy. He's sitting near a cauldron.</p>

<p>"Who's that?" Merlin thinks.</p>

<p>"Come here, boy!" the enchantress cries.</p>

<p>"I need to run!" Merlin thinks. In his head, he sees a hair. Then suddenly he is a hair.</p>

<p>"Aha," he thinks. "So now I can change into different animals."</p>

<p>Merlin runs away into the forest. "I can easily run faster than Keridwen now," he thinks.</p>

<p>But when he looks behind him, he sees a big dog. It is running after him and its angry mouth is open.</p>

<p>"Oh no!" he thinks. "Keridwen can change into different animals too."</p>

<p>Merlin moves quickly through the trees. But he can hear Keridwen behind him. She is coming nearer.</p>

<p>Just then, he arrives at a lake. "I need to be a fish," he thinks. Suddenly, he's a long, dark fish. He moves fast through the cold water of the lake.</p>

<p>"I'm all right now," he thinks.</p>

<p>But when he looks back, an otter is coming through the water behind him. "Keridwen's an otter now," he thinks. So he changes into a little bird and goes far up into the sky.</p>

<p>But soon, he hears an angry cry behind him. Now a big eagle is coming after him.</p>

<p>"Keridwen again," Merlin thinks. He moves here and there in the sky. But he can't lose the eagle.</p>

<p>Just then, Merlin sees a hunter far down in the forest. And there's a sudden noise. An arrow hits Keridwen. She falls to the ground near her house.</p>

<p>Morfran runs to her dead body.</p>

<p>"Merlin, you demon!" he cries angrily. "One day, you must die for this!"</p>

<summary>Chapter 4</summary>

<div class="book-page">
  <div class="chapter-label">Chapter 4</div>
  <div class="chapter-title">Learning Magic</div>
  <div class="chapter-divider"><span> ~ ~ ~ </span></div>
  <div class="book-body">
    <p class="first-para">After some years, Princess Adhan, Merlin's mother, moves away to a convent. But Merlin stays in his father's castle. He reads books and works on his magic powers.</p>

<p>"I want to be the best magician in the kingdom," he says.</p>

<p>At first, Merlin learns to change the weather. Next, he learns to see through walls. Then, he learns to look into the future.</p>

<p>Morfran lives with his father in the forest. He often thinks about Merlin.</p>

<p>"Perhaps I can kill him with one of my mother's old potions," Morfran tells his friend, Arwell.</p>

<p>But the old potion bottles have nothing in them. All but one.</p>

<p>"And this potion doesn't kill people," Morfran says. "It makes ill people well again. So give it to somebody important," Arwell answers, "and pretend to be a magician."</p>

<p>Soon after that, King Vortigern visits Wales after years of fighting. He has a new wife, Ronwen. But she is ill.</p>

<p>Morfran hears about this. He goes to the king's men with the magic potion. Ronwen is well again after she drinks it. So Vortigern calls for Morfran.</p>

<p>"You can be my magician," the king says.</p>

<summary>Chapter 5</summary>

<div class="book-page">
  <div class="chapter-label">Chapter 5</div>
  <div class="chapter-title">The King's Castle</div>
  <div class="chapter-divider"><span> ~ ~ ~ </span></div>
  <div class="book-body">
    <p class="first-para">Ronwen is happy in Wales. She wants to stay there.</p>

<p>"Let's make a castle on this hill," she tells Vortigern.</p>

<p>Vortigern begins the new castle. But the walls fall down again and again.</p>

<p>"Make a magic potion and stop this," the king tells Morfran.</p>

<p>"I need the blood of a boy without a father for that," Morfran answers. "Now, the son of a demon lives near here. Give me some men and I can bring him to you."</p>

<p>Vortigern's men bring Merlin to the hill.</p>

<p>"Here's the demon's son," Morfran tells Vortigern. "Kill him. I can make a potion with his blood and put it on the ground. Then you can finish your castle."</p>

<p>"Morfran's my enemy and he's wrong!" Merlin cries. "There are two dragons under the hill. I can see them through the ground."</p>

<p>"Dragons?" Vortigern asks.</p>

<p>"Yes," Merlin cries. "A red dragon and a white dragon. When they fight, the walls fall down."</p>

<p>The dragons hear Merlin's cries. And they come up angrily through the ground. They are fighting and breathing fire and smoke.</p>

<summary>Chapter 6</summary>

<div class="book-page">
  <div class="chapter-label">Chapter 6</div>
  <div class="chapter-title">A True Magician</div>
  <div class="chapter-divider"><span> ~ ~ ~ </span></div>
  <div class="book-body">
    <p class="first-para">"I can stop them," Morfran says.</p>

<p>He pretends to do some magic. But he is not a true magician. The dragons breathe fire on Morfran and kill him.</p>

<p>Merlin quickly changes the weather. Suddenly, there's a wall of rain in front of the dragons. They move up into the sky. When the rain goes, the dragons are far away.</p>

<p>"You're a true magician!" Vortigern cries.</p>

<p>He finishes his castle and calls for Merlin.</p>

<p>"Tell me about the future," the king says.</p>

<p>Merlin closes his eyes. "You don't have to worry about the future. Vortigern will die without any children," he answers. "Dark years of fighting come after that. Then I see a sword with magic light in it. A king with a wonderful castle and his beautiful wife. I'm that king's magician."</p>

<p>"Be my magician!" Vortigern cries.</p>

<p>"No," Merlin answers. "I must work more on my magic. I must be ready when my king and my country need me."</p>

<p>Then Merlin changes into a hair and runs away into the forest.</p>

* Где живёт юный Мерлин и с кем?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Он живёт в замке в Уэльсе (Wales) со своей матерью — принцессой Адан (Princess Adhan).
  </details>

* Кто единственный друг Мерлина?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Жаба (a toad).
  </details>

* Как зовут двух мальчиков, которые дразнят Мерлина, и кто из них сын волшебницы?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Их зовут Морфран (Morfran) и Арвел (Arwel). Морфран — сын волшебницы Керидвен (Ceridwen).
  </details>

* Что отец Мерлина советует ему во сне?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Он советует: «Когда они тебя дразнят, уходи. Ищи самый большой дуб в лесу. Возле него есть что-то важное».
  </details>

* Что варит Морфран в котле и для чего?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Он варит зелье (potion), которое даёт магические силы первому, кто его выпьет. Керидвен хочет, чтобы эти силы получил Морфран.
  </details>

* Как Мерлин случайно пробует зелье?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Он выпрыгивает, чтобы отнять жабу у Морфрана, ударяет ногой котёл, зелье падает ему на руку, и он облизывает его.
  </details>

* Какую способность получает Мерлин после зелья?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Он может превращаться в разных животных (change into different animals). Сначала он превращается в зайца (hare).
  </details>

* В кого превращается Керидвен, чтобы догнать Мерлина?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Сначала в большую собаку (big dog), потом в выдру (otter), потом в орла (eagle).
  </details>

* Кто убивает Керидвен и как?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Охотник (hunter) стреляет в неё из лука, когда она в облике орла. Стрела попадает в неё.
  </details>

* Кто такой король Вортигерн и чем больна его новая жена Ронвен?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Вортигерн — король. Его жена Ронвен больна (she is ill). Морфран вылечивает её своим зельем и становится королевским магом.
  </details>

* Почему стены нового замка Вортигерна всё время падают?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Потому что под холмом сражаются два дракона — красный и белый (a red dragon and a white dragon).
  </details>

* Что происходит с Морфраном в конце?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Драконы дышат на него огнём и убивают его (breathe fire on Morfran and kill him).
  </details>

* Какое будущее предсказывает Мерлин королю Вортигерну?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Он говорит: «Ты умрёшь без детей. После этого наступят тёмные годы войны. А затем я увижу меч с магическим светом, короля с прекрасным замком и его красивую жену. Я буду магом того короля».
  </details>

* Что Мерлин делает в конце истории?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Он снова превращается в зайца и убегает в лес.
  </details>
