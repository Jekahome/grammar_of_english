  <div class="book-body">
    <p class="first-para">"We don't want to stay with Uncle Henry, Mum." The boy with dark hair and blue eyes takes his mobile phone out of his pocket. "Grandma says he doesn't like teenagers."</p>

<p>His mother shakes her head and Tom looks out of the window again. The car goes left. The sign on the gate says, "Manor Hall".</p>

<p>"Manor Hall," says the beautiful girl sitting next to her brother Tom. "Does Uncle Henry live here?"</p>

<p>Her mother hears what Millie says. "Henry is strange, but he's nice too."</p>

<p>"Can't we come to the new house with you, Mum? We can help," Millie asks.</p>

<p>"No, Millie," says her mum. "There aren't any beds in the new house."</p>

<p>The car stops. "Here we are," says Mum. The house is very old and dark. There are lots of small windows. Millie is afraid.</p>

<p>A man opens the door. He is old and he has got white hair and big eyebrows. He looks at Tom and Millie with his grey eyes.</p>

<p>"You're late," he says. "Come in."</p>

<p>"Sorry," says Tom. "I'm Tom. How do you do?" The old man looks angry.</p>

<p>"And I'm Millie," says Millie. "Your house is very big."</p>

<p>"It is," says Uncle Henry. "You can explore, but there is one room where you can't go."</p>

<p>They walk along a corridor and see a sitting room where there are beautiful sofas and pictures on the walls. Then they come to a door.</p>

<p>"This is the music room," says Uncle Henry. There is a sign on the door. It says, "DO NOT ENTER".</p>

<p>"Why can't we go in there?" asks Millie. "I can play the piano and I love music."</p>

<p>Uncle Henry shakes his head. "I can't tell you, but never open this door."</p>

<p>"What a strange man," Tom says when they are in their bedroom. "Why can't we go into the music room?"</p>

<p>But Millie isn't listening. "Let's explore," she says.</p>

<p>Tom and Millie walk up and down and open doors. Then they come to the music room. Tom is about to open the door when Uncle Henry sees them.</p>

<p>"Get away from there!" shouts Uncle Henry.</p>

<p>Later, when they are having dinner in the dining room, Uncle Henry is very quiet.</p>

<p>"Are you angry with us?" asks Millie. Uncle Henry doesn't answer. He shakes his head. Then he says, "There's a full moon tonight." He gets up and looks out of the window. "Lock your bedroom door," he says. "And don't open it."</p>

<p>"I don't understand," Millie says to Tom. "Why does Uncle Henry look at the moon? Why can't we go into the music room? It's a mystery."</p>

<p>Tom isn't listening. He's reading a book.</p>

<p>"I want to explore," Tom says suddenly.</p>

<p>"We can't," says Millie.</p>

    <p class="first-para">It is midnight and Millie wakes up. It's very dark in the bedroom and she can hear a noise. She gets out of bed and goes to her brother's bed.</p>

<p>"Tom? Tom," she says, and shakes his arm. Tom opens his eyes.</p>

<p>"Millie, it's late. Go back to bed." He closes his eyes again.</p>

<p>"Now, Tom, get up. There's a strange noise."</p>

<p>Tom goes to the door and listens. "You're right," he says to Millie. "I can hear someone singing."</p>

<p>"Can Uncle Henry sing?" Millie asks Tom. He laughs. "Let's see." He opens the door.</p>

<p>"No," Millie says. "There's a full moon. We can't go out."</p>

<p>"You can stay here, but I want to see who's singing at midnight."</p>

<p>Tom opens the door and they run into the corridor.</p>

<p>"Someone is singing in the music room," says Millie.</p>

<p>"You're right," says Tom. "It's a man and he's singing a song about the sea. It's beautiful," says Millie. "Is it Uncle Henry?"</p>

<p>Tom isn't listening. He opens the door of the music room and goes in.</p>

<p>"Don't, Tom," says Millie. "We can't go into the music room."</p>

  <div class="book-body">
    <p class="first-para">The music room is very big and Millie is afraid. "We haven't got much time before Uncle Henry hears us," she says. Tom puts the light on and the singing stops.</p>

<p>In the music room there's a piano and there are lots of pictures on the wall. But Uncle Henry isn't there. Millie wants to go. On one wall there is a very big picture of a ship. Millie and Tom look at the picture and they can see that the sea is moving. They can hear the water.</p>

<p>There is a man standing on the ship. He has got long black hair and a black hat. He is singing a song about the sea. He's a pirate.</p>

<p>Tom and Millie are afraid but they can't move. The pirate looks at them. He stops singing and he smiles. Suddenly he comes out of the picture and into the music room.</p>

<p>"Help!" shouts Millie.</p>

<p>Millie looks at the handsome pirate. He has a big hat and he is very white. "He's a ghost," she says in a quiet voice to Tom. "Tom, let's go." Tom doesn't answer. The pirate is near the door so they can't go.</p>

<p>"Who's in my music room?" the pirate shouts. "This is my room."</p>

<p>"You're right," says Tom. "This is your music room and we're going to bed."</p>

<p>The pirate shakes his head. "Oh no, you're not. Sit down. I'm going to tell you my story."</p>

  <div class="book-body">
    <p class="first-para">"My name is William Bones but everyone calls me Billy," says the pirate. "The year is 1793. My men and I sail the seven seas in my ship, the Pearl. We fight pirate ships and we sing songs about the sea. We are very happy because we have lots of treasure.</p>

<p>"Then one day I help an old woman and she paints a picture of my ship. 'This picture's magic,' she tells me. 'Before you die, put it in a house near the sea. When you die and the moon is full, your ghost can sail your ship for one night.' Then the old woman looks at me and she says, 'Pirate Billy Bones, remember that the picture must be near the sea.'</p>

<p>"Now it's 1850," Billy says. "When I'm old, I die in my house by the sea. The old woman is right. When the moon is full, my ghost can sail my ship for one night.</p>

<p>"But then, in 1910, someone brings the picture here to Manor Hall," the pirate says. "This house isn't near the sea, so I can't sail my ship. When the moon is full, I sing songs about the sea. And the people in the house are afraid. They are afraid of the ghost. They put my picture in the music room and lock the door."</p>

<p>Suddenly, the door of the music room opens. Uncle Henry stands there. He looks at Billy Bones and then at Tom and Millie. His face is white.</p>

<p>"Oh dear," he says, "now you know about the mystery."</p>

<div class="book-page">
  <div class="chapter-label">Chapter 5</div>
  <div class="chapter-title"></div>
  <div class="chapter-divider"><span> ~ ~ ~ </span></div>
  <div class="book-body">
    <p class="first-para">"Now, I must tell you my story," says Uncle Henry. "The year is 1976. I buy this house from an old man. He tells me about the picture and the ghost in the music room. I'm afraid of ghosts and I don't go into the music room. I lock the door.</p>

<p>"Every month, when there is a full moon, I hear a pirate singing songs about the sea," says Uncle Henry. "I know that he is unhappy, and I know that he wants to go back to the sea. But I'm afraid, and I can't help him."</p>

<p>"I know," says Tom. "Our new house is in a beautiful place near the sea," he says. "From my bedroom, I can hear the seagulls and the waves. Come with us. We can put your picture in the breakfast room where you can see the sea."</p>

<p>Uncle Henry begins to smile.</p>

<p>"How wonderful," says Billy Bones.</p>

<p>"I'm happy that you want to go with Tom and Millie," says Uncle Henry. "Please sit down, Billy Bones, and listen." Everyone sits at the kitchen table. "You can go, but you must be quiet. You must sing quietly in their new house."</p>

<p>"All right," says Billy Bones.</p>

<p>"Are you afraid of ghosts now, Henry?" Henry smiles. "No, I'm not, Billy."</p>

<p>"Mum's here," says Millie. They run out of the house. "Mum, we have something from Uncle Henry," says Millie. "It's for the new house."</p>

<p>Mum looks at the picture, a ship, and a handsome pirate. "How lovely," says Mum.</p>

<p>Tom and Millie say goodbye to Uncle Henry. They take the picture to their new house by the sea. Pirate Billy Bones stands on his ship and he looks at his new home. He can hear seagulls and he quietly sings a song about the sea. One day, when there is a full moon, his ship disappears.</p>

* Как зовут брата и сестру, которых мама оставляет у дяди Генри?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Том (Tom) и Милли (Milly).
  </details>

* Что дядя Генри запрещает детям делать?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Он запрещает им заходить в музыкальную комнату (music room). На двери висит табличка «Не входить» (Do Not Enter).
  </details>

* Что дядя Генри говорит сделать в ночь полнолуния?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Он говорит запереть дверь спальни (Lock your bedroom door) и не открывать её.
  </details>

* Что Том и Милли слышат в полночь из музыкальной комнаты?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Они слышат пение (someone singing). Мужчина поёт песню о море.
  </details>

* Кого они видят в музыкальной комнате?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Они видят пирата (a pirate), который выходит из картины с кораблём. Это призрак (ghost).
  </details>

* Как зовут пирата и как называется его корабль?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Его зовут Уильям Боунс (William Bones), но все называют его Билли (Billy). Его корабль называется «Жемчужина» (The Pearl).
  </details>

* Какую тайну рассказывает Билли о картине?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Одна старушка нарисовала его корабль и сказала: «Перед смертью помести картину в дом у моря. Когда ты умрёшь и будет полнолуние, твой призрак сможет плавать на корабле одну ночь».
  </details>

* Почему Билли не может плавать на корабле в Manor Hall?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Потому что Manor Hall находится не у моря (isn't near the sea).
  </details>

* Какую идею предлагает Том?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Он предлагает забрать картину в их новый дом, который стоит у моря (near the sea), и повесить её в комнате для завтрака (breakfast room).
  </details>

* Что происходит в конце истории?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Том и Милли забирают картину в новый дом у моря. Билли Боунс тихо поёт песню о море. Однажды в полнолуние его корабль исчезает (his ship disappears).
  </details>
