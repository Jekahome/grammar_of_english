<summary>Chapter One</summary>

<div class="book-page">
  <div class="chapter-label">Chapter One</div>
  <div class="chapter-title">Early Days</div>
  <div class="chapter-divider"><span> ~ ~ ~ </span></div>
  <div class="book-body">
    <p class="first-para">People in every country know that name. They know his face. They know his number, 23. He is tall and strong. He has a lot of money. He is very famous. And he can fly.</p>

<p>Michael Jordan is a basketball player. He played for thirteen years for the Chicago Bulls. Many people understand basketball. A lot of people love basketball. Michael Jordan lives basketball. Every game is important to him. But where did he come from? How did he win six NBA championships? When did he start to fly?</p>

<p>Michael's story starts on February 17, 1963, in Brooklyn, New York. The family moves to Wilmington, North Carolina later in 1963. Michael is the son of Dolores and James Jordan. He comes from a happy home with a lot of love. One old friend says, "The Jordans' door was always open. Mr. and Mrs. Jordan talked to us and listened to us. There was always a basketball game in the backyard."</p>

<p>One of Michael's best friends is his brother, Larry. Larry teaches his little brother a lot of things about basketball. Mr. Jordan builds a big basketball court for his sons and their friends. This is Michael's first basketball school. Today, Michael says, "We played basketball games every day. On Saturdays, we were on our court all day. Rain? No problem. We played basketball."</p>

<p>For a long time, Larry is the best basketball player in the Jordan family. Baseball is Michael's game. He is strong and quick. He always wants to win. The Jordan children have jobs at home, but Michael doesn't like working. His brothers and sisters have money for things. Michael doesn't have any money because he doesn't work. The only jobs in his life are baseball and basketball.</p>

<summary>Chapter Two</summary>

<div class="book-page">
  <div class="chapter-label">Chapter Two</div>
  <div class="chapter-title">Basketball at School</div>
  <div class="chapter-divider"><span> ~ ~ ~ </span></div>
  <div class="book-body">
    <p class="first-para">From twelve to fifteen years old, Michael plays baseball, basketball, and football at school. He is short for a basketball player. He is only 1.75 metres — 5 feet 9 inches. Mr. and Mrs. Jordan are under 1.75 metres. Michael isn't going to be very tall.</p>

<p>In 1978, Michael starts his three years at Laney High School. His teachers say now, "Michael was a good student. He was quiet, but he had a lot of friends." Michael starts basketball at Laney on the A team. Larry is on the A team too. He has number 45. Michael asks for number 23 because 45 divided by 2 equals 22 and a half.</p>

<p>But Michael doesn't stay on the A team. The coach moves him to the B team. Michael is angry. He wants to be on the best team. He plays basketball every morning, afternoon, and evening. Sometimes he doesn't go to school. His father talks to him. "Basketball is important, but school is important too." Michael goes back to school.</p>

<p>It is the summer of 1979. Mr. Jordan remembers, "Michael wanted to be tall. He wanted to be on the A team." Suddenly, he was 1.91 metres — 6 feet 3 inches tall. In September, Michael plays on the A team. He is the star of basketball at Laney. The A team games start at 7.30 in the evening. Before 5 o'clock, the people of Wilmington are waiting at the court. They want to see Michael. Laney wins its first championship that year.</p>

<summary>Chapter Three</summary>

<div class="book-page">
  <div class="chapter-label">Chapter Three</div>
  <div class="chapter-title">College Life</div>
  <div class="chapter-divider"><span> ~ ~ ~ </span></div>
  <div class="book-body">
    <p class="first-para">Now, Michael thinks about basketball after Laney. A lot of colleges want him. Michael likes the University of North Carolina, UNC in Chapel Hill. He likes the basketball coach, Dean Smith.</p>

<p>Michael goes to UNC in September 1981, with his sister Roslyn. She is a very good student, and she starts college early. Dean Smith teaches Michael a lot of things about basketball. "Remember, every player on the team is important," he says. Michael works on his game. He is on the first team in his first year at UNC. Michael is the best new player in college basketball in 1981-82.</p>

<p>The UNC team, the Tar Heels, get to the 1982 college basketball championships. They are going to play the Georgetown Hoyas. There are 61,000 people in New Orleans for the big game. It is Tar Heels 61, Hoyas 62. There is only a little time in the game now. Dean Smith says to his players, "Get the ball to Michael." Michael has the ball. The Hoyas run at him, but Michael doesn't look at the players. He looks at the basket. He takes a long shot. The ball is in the basket. UNC wins its first college championship.</p>

<p>The story of "The Shot" is in the newspapers and on TV. Michael is famous. His photo and "The Shot" are on the Chapel Hill telephone book in 1982.</p>

<summary>Chapter Four</summary>

<div class="book-page">
  <div class="chapter-label">Chapter Four</div>
  <div class="chapter-title">The NBA, 1984 to 1993</div>
  <div class="chapter-divider"><span> ~ ~ ~ </span></div>
  <div class="book-body">
    <p class="first-para">Every year, the teams in the NBA look for good new players. After three important and happy years at UNC, Michael Jordan thinks about the NBA. But Dolores Jordan says to her son, "Finish your four years at UNC. It's very important." Michael says, "I'm going to go to the NBA. But I'm going to finish my college work too." Michael finishes his work at UNC in 1986. His mother is very happy.</p>

<p>In June of 1984, the NBA teams are looking for tall players. Michael is 1.98 metres — 6 feet 6 inches. He isn't very tall for the NBA. The Houston Rockets take Hakeem Olajuwon, 2.08 metres — 6 feet 10 inches. The Portland Trail Blazers take Sam Bowie, 2.16 metres — 7 feet 1 inch. Then, at number three, Jordan hears his name. The Chicago Bulls want him.</p>

<p>The Bulls aren't a good team in 1984. They win only 27 of 82 games, but they give Michael $6 million for seven years. The Bulls always say to their players, "You play for us now. No basketball on your vacations. This is your job." But Michael wants to play with his friends and with Larry. The Bulls want Michael. They say, "OK, Michael, you can play for us and you can play for the love of the game too."</p>

<p>In July of 1984, Michael catches an airplane to Los Angeles. He is on the 1984 U.S. Olympic basketball team. The best college players in the country are on the team. They win their games and the Olympic award for basketball. Michael gives his award to his mother. He plays again and wins in the 1992 Olympic Games.</p>

<p>After the 1984 Olympic Games, Michael starts his job with the Bulls. In the NBA, he can be a big star on the basketball court. Nike makes shoes for basketball players. "Michael from Nike" say, "This new star can fly. He's going to be very famous." Nike makes a shoe for Michael. Its name is Air Jordan. Now Michael plays and people say, "Can he fly — or is it the shoes?" People buy a lot of Air Jordan shoes. In two years, Nike makes $130 million from them. Michael makes a lot of money from them too.</p>

<p>Michael loves the NBA. His game is strong. People like watching him. But life away from the basketball court is often difficult. People always want to talk to him. Michael can't walk down the street. He can't go to a store or to a movie. People want to be his friend.</p>

<p>In 1985, Michael meets Juanita Vanoy. She is smart and beautiful and interesting. But she doesn't know about basketball. She likes Michael because he is a good person. They want the same things in life.</p>

<p>From 1984 to 1987, Michael wins a lot of awards in the NBA. But he wants to win the NBA championship. The newspapers ask, "Is Jordan a team player? Can the Bulls win the championship?"</p>

<p>From 1987 to 1990, the Bulls get some new players and a new coach, Phil Jackson. The Bulls start to build a championship team. In December 1988, there is a very happy story in the newspapers. Michael and Juanita have their first son. His name is Jeffrey.</p>

<p>The 1990-91 Bulls are very good. They win 61 of 82 games. The Bulls and the Los Angeles Lakers are in the championship games. The Bulls win the championship in five games. Michael says, "We're a strong team. After seven years, we did it." Now, no team can stop the Bulls. They win the championship in 1992 and in 1993. The newspapers say, "Michael Jordan is the best. He can win every game."</p>

<p>Michael has many awards and three championships in three years. He and Juanita now have two sons, Jeffrey and Marcus, and one daughter, Jasmine. After the championships in 1993, Michael goes home with his family. He is strong and happy.</p>

<summary>Chapter Five</summary>

<div class="book-page">
  <div class="chapter-label">Chapter Five</div>
  <div class="chapter-title">A New Baseball Star?</div>
  <div class="chapter-divider"><span> ~ ~ ~ </span></div>
  <div class="book-body">
    <p class="first-para">Things are good for the Jordan family in the summer of 1993. But then, one day, Michael's father visits some friends. That evening, he doesn't come home. The police find him in a river. Two eighteen-year-old boys killed him for his car and his money. Michael loved his father very much and he can't understand this. Why did these boys kill a good man?</p>

<p>On October 6, 1993, Michael and Juanita are on TV. Michael says, "Life is very short. I want to have time with my family." Michael retires from basketball. The Bulls retire his number, 23.</p>

<p>Early in 1994, Michael starts a new job in baseball. Michael and a lot of young players try for a place on the Chicago White Sox baseball team. Michael can run well. He can catch well. But he can't hit a baseball very well. The newspapers say, "Mike, please come back to basketball." But Michael likes playing baseball. In baseball, he isn't a star. Michael's number is 45 — Larry's old number.</p>

<p>But in August 1994, there are problems in baseball about money. The players stop the games. They don't play for a long time. Michael is thirty-one years old. He can't wait for baseball.</p>

<summary>Chapter Six</summary>

<div class="book-page">
  <div class="chapter-label">Chapter Six</div>
  <div class="chapter-title">The NBA, 1995 to 1998</div>
  <div class="chapter-divider"><span> ~ ~ ~ </span></div>
  <div class="book-body">
    <p class="first-para">On March 8, 1995, the Chicago Bulls office gives a story to the newspapers. It is a short story from Michael Jordan. It says, "I'm back." Chicago is a very happy town that night.</p>

<p>Michael is happy with the Bulls again and with his old friends and his coach, Phil Jackson. The team works well and starts to win again. Michael is number 45 in the championship games. The Bulls aren't doing very well. Some players say, "Number 45 is OK. But number 23 was a star." This makes Michael angry. He goes back to number 23. Now the players remember — he's back.</p>

<p>The Bulls don't win the championship in 1995. In the summer, Michael has some time. He makes a movie, Space Jam. Children love it. It makes a lot of money.</p>

<p>The Bulls start 1995-96 with a new player, Dennis Rodman, and some old players, Scottie Pippen and Michael Jordan. The Bulls are the best team in the NBA again. And Michael Jordan is their star. The Bulls win the NBA championship in 1996, 1997, and 1998. Michael Jordan has six NBA championship awards and he has basketball awards in every room in his house.</p>

<p>But there are always problems in life. The Chicago Bulls office wants a new coach. But Michael Jordan likes playing for Phil Jackson. The NBA players are unhappy about their money too. They start the games late in 1998. Then Michael and Juanita are on TV again. Michael retires from basketball on January 13, 1999.</p>

<p>He has good times and bad times in his first thirty-six years. But we remember Michael Jordan because he can fly.</p>

<p>Michael has a lot of money from his life in basketball. In 2000, he buys an NBA team, the Washington Wizards. They aren't a good team. Michael Jordan isn't a player this time. It is his team. This is an interesting time for basketball. Is Michael Jordan's team going to fly?</p>

* Где и когда родился Майкл Джордан?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Он родился 17 февраля 1963 года в Бруклине, Нью-Йорк (Brooklyn, New York).
  </details>

* Кто был лучшим другом Майкла в детстве и чему он его научил?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Его лучший друг — брат Ларри (Larry). Он научил Майкла многому о баскетболе.
  </details>

* Почему тренер перевёл Майкла из команды «А» в команду «Б» в старшей школе?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Потому что он был слишком низким (short) — всего 1.75 метра.
  </details>

* Какой номер Майкл выбрал в школе и почему?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Он выбрал номер 23, потому что 45 (номер Ларри) разделить на 2 — примерно 22,5.
  </details>

* Какое решение изменило карьеру Майкла в баскетболе?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Летом 1979 года он внезапно вырос до 1.91 метра (6'3).
  </details>

* Какой университет выбрал Майкл и кто был его тренером?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Он выбрал Университет Северной Каролины (University of North Carolina) в Чапел-Хилл. Тренера звали Дин Смит (Dean Smith).
  </details>

* Какой решающий бросок (the shot) принёс UNC победу в чемпионате колледжей?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Майкл сделал дальний бросок (long shot), и мяч попал в корзину (ball in the basket).
  </details>

* Какая компания создала кроссовки «Air Jordan» и сколько на них заработала?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Компания Nike. За два года она заработала 130 миллионов долларов.
  </details>

* Сколько чемпионатов НБА выиграл Майкл Джордан за свою карьеру и с какой командой?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Он выиграл шесть чемпионатов (six championships) с командой «Чикаго Буллз» (Chicago Bulls) в 1991, 1992, 1993, 1996, 1997 и 1998 годах.
  </details>

* Что случилось с отцом Майкла летом 1993 года?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Два восемнадцатилетних парня убили его (killed him) ради машины и денег.
  </details>

* Почему Майкл ушёл из баскетбола в 1993 году?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Он сказал: «Жизнь очень коротка. Я хочу проводить время с семьёй» (Life is very short. I want to have time with my family).
  </details>

* В каком фильме снялся Майкл Джордан в 1995 году?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  В фильме «Космический джем» (Space Jam).
  </details>

* Что Майкл сделал в 2000 году?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Он купил команду НБА «Вашингтон Уизардс» (Washington Wizards).
  </details>
