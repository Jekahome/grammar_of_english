<div class="book-page">
  <div class="chapter-label">Chapter One</div>
  <div class="chapter-title">Young Jennifer</div>
  <div class="chapter-divider"><span> ~ ~ ~ </span></div>
  <div class="book-body">
    <p class="first-para">Jennifer Lopez. She can dance. She can sing. She is a smart business woman. She is beautiful and she is Hollywood's number one Latina movie star. But the story of Jennifer Lopez starts in New York.</p>

<p>It is July 24, 1970. A hot summer day in the Bronx, New York. A lot of Puerto Rican people live here and they are not all happy. But it is a good day for David and Guadalupe Lopez and their daughters Linda and Leslie. They are happy because they have a new little girl in the family — Jennifer. They hold her and they love her.</p>

<p>Sometimes she is a singer. Sometimes she is a dancer or a movie star. She loves the movie West Side Story. She watches it a hundred times. In West Side Story, Rita Moreno plays Anita. For Jennifer, Rita is America's number one actress and dancer.</p>

<p>"I'd like to be her," Jennifer thinks.</p>

<p>At five, Jennifer goes to a school of theatre and dance. In a short time, she can sing, act and dance very well. But is this important? David and Guadalupe ask. They have good jobs. They want to see Jennifer in a good job too. For this, schoolwork is very important.</p>

<summary>Chapter Two</summary>

<div class="book-page">
  <div class="chapter-label">Chapter Two</div>
  <div class="chapter-title">Jennifer the Dancer</div>
  <div class="chapter-divider"><span> ~ ~ ~ </span></div>
  <div class="book-body">
    <p class="first-para">In 1975, Jennifer goes to the Holy Family Catholic School in New York. It is a good school and she is a good student. But in 1983, the problems start. Now, schoolwork is not very important to Jennifer. She wants to be a performer.</p>

<p>One day, in 1988, she says, "I want to stop going to school." David and Guadalupe are angry. Jennifer is angry with them too. She walks out of the house.</p>

<p>Jennifer lives in her dance school for a short time. Then she moves into an apartment in Manhattan with some friends. She dances in the school every day. She works well. Then she starts to look for a job.</p>

<p>She gets a job in the theatre show Golden Musicals of Broadway. The show goes to Europe. Then, after five months, she gets a job in a new show, Synchronicity. This show goes to Japan. In 1991, Jennifer is back in the U.S. She is a very good dancer now and she is beautiful too.</p>

<p>A lot of famous singers want the young dancer for their short music movies. Jennifer dances behind the singer Janet Jackson. This helps her and she meets important people in the movie business. There are no problems with her family now. She has work and she is a success. She has a boyfriend too, David Cruz.</p>

<p>Later that year, Jennifer moves to Los Angeles for the famous TV show In Living Color. Jennifer is a dancer in the show but she is not always happy in her job. She wants to be an actress.</p>

<summary>Chapter Three</summary>

<div class="book-page">
  <div class="chapter-label">Chapter Three</div>
  <div class="chapter-title">Jennifer the Actress</div>
  <div class="chapter-divider"><span> ~ ~ ~ </span></div>
  <div class="book-body">
    <p class="first-para">The head of the TV show South Central likes Jennifer's work. In 1993, he wants an actress for the show. He gives the job to Jennifer. After South Central, Jennifer works in three new TV shows: Second Chances, Malibu Road, and Nurses on the Line: The Crash of Flight 7. Jennifer is now a famous TV star.</p>

<p>Then, in 1995, she is in her first Hollywood movie, Mi Familia. Mi Familia is a Latin movie. Her first English movies are Money Train and Jack.</p>

<p>In Money Train, Jennifer is Grace Santiago, a New York police woman. Wesley Snipes and Woody Harrelson play two brothers. They work for the police too. The two men love Grace. The movie is about their problems.</p>

<p>Jack is a funny movie. Robin Williams plays a boy, Jack. People look at Jack and think, "He's not a boy, he's a man." Jennifer plays Jack's teacher.</p>

<p>In her movies, Jennifer is in love stories with famous men. Her boyfriend, David, is unhappy about this, and he goes back to New York. Jennifer is unhappy too. But then, she finds a new boyfriend. His name is Ojani Noa, and he is from Cuba. She marries him in 1997.</p>

<p>In the same year, Jennifer is in four movies. The first movie is Blood and Wine, with two very famous actors, Jack Nicholson and Michael Caine. The movie is about two bad men, Alex Gates and Victor Spansky. Jennifer is Gabriella, Alex's girlfriend.</p>

<p>After Blood and Wine, Jennifer is in the movies Anaconda, U-turn and Selena. The movies are a success in the U.S., but Selena is a success in Latin countries too. In Selena, Jennifer plays Selena Quintanilla, the famous Mexican singer. It is an unhappy story because the singer's life was very short. Selena's father gives money for the movie. The director is Gregory Nava.</p>

<p>Jennifer works on the movie in front of Selena's family. This is difficult, but the family likes Jennifer, and the movie teaches people about their daughter. This makes Jennifer happy because she liked Selena. Selena is a big success in many countries. Suddenly, Jennifer Lopez is not only a famous name in the U.S.</p>

<p>The year 1998 arrives. Jennifer is happy, but she can't take a vacation. She is in two movies that year, Out of Sight and Ants.</p>

<p>In Out of Sight, Jennifer is Karen Sisco, a police woman. George Clooney is Jack Foley. Foley takes money from a bank. He gets away from the police and takes Karen with him. The story is about their love. Ants is a movie for children. We hear Jennifer, but we don't see her face.</p>

<summary>Chapter Four</summary>

<div class="book-page">
  <div class="chapter-label">Chapter Four</div>
  <div class="chapter-title">Men and Movies</div>
  <div class="chapter-divider"><span> ~ ~ ~ </span></div>
  <div class="book-body">
    <p class="first-para">Jennifer's 1998 movies are a big success, but Ojani isn't happy. He and David have the same problem. They don't like to see Jennifer with men in love stories. Ojani moves away. Later, he and Jennifer divorce, but they meet often and they are good friends.</p>

<p>In 1999, Jennifer has a new boyfriend. His name is Sean Combs. He is a famous black singer. On December 27, 1999, Sean and Jennifer come out of a dance bar and get in their car. The police stop them. They find a gun in the front of the car. The gun is not Sean's, but the police take him away. Jennifer is in the back of the car. It is not her problem. The story is in the newspapers. Early in 2000, Jennifer stops seeing Sean.</p>

<p>In that year, she makes the movie The Cell. Jennifer plays Catherine Deane, a doctor. Vincent D'Onofrio plays Carl, a bad man. He takes a young girl, but where is she?</p>

<p>In 2001, Jennifer stars in The Wedding Planner. This is a funny movie and a love story. Jennifer plays Mary Fiore. Mary falls in love with Steve Edison, a doctor. The Wedding Planner is the number one movie of 2001.</p>

<p>In the same year, Jennifer marries Chris Judd, a dancer, but they divorce in August 2002. Jennifer stars in two thrillers, Angel Eyes and Enough. Then she is in Maid in Manhattan, a love story.</p>

<p>Jennifer starts going out with the actor Ben Affleck. In 2003, they star in the movie Gigli. A lot of newspapers don't like the movie, but Gigli makes money. Are Ben and Jennifer going to marry? People ask. The answer is no. Jennifer's love story finishes in September.</p>

<p>But in 2004, Ben and Jennifer act in Jersey Girl, the story of a father and his little girl. Jennifer is not the star of that movie. After Jersey Girl comes Shall We Dance with Richard Gere. It is a love story about a dance teacher and her student. Jennifer sings for it with her friend, Marc Anthony.</p>

<p>Some people are unhappy about Jennifer's movies at this time. "Jennifer doesn't act well, and the stories aren't interesting," they say. But 2004 is a good year for Jennifer. She falls in love with Marc. Jennifer and Marc marry, and they are very happy.</p>

<p>After the movies Monster-in-Law, An Unfinished Life, and Bordertown, Jennifer and Marc star in El Cantante. Marc plays the famous Puerto Rican singer, Hector Lavoe. Lavoe married Puchi, and Jennifer plays her.</p>

<summary>Chapter Five</summary>

<div class="book-page">
  <div class="chapter-label">Chapter Five</div>
  <div class="chapter-title">Jennifer the Singer</div>
  <div class="chapter-divider"><span> ~ ~ ~ </span></div>
  <div class="book-body">
    <p class="first-para">Music was always important to Jennifer. Today, the famous actress is a famous singer too. Jennifer's music success story starts in 1999. In that year, she makes On the 6. It is her first music CD. The name comes from the subway station near her home in New York. On the CD, she sings "No Me Ames" with Marc Anthony. She sings in English too, but the music is Latin. After only five weeks, "If You Had My Love" from the same CD is number one in the U.S.</p>

<p>The success of Jennifer the singer doesn't stop. In 2001, her CD J.Lo is again number one in the U.S. She is the first singer-actress with a CD and a movie — The Wedding Planner — at number one in the same week.</p>

<p>In 2002, Jennifer makes two music CDs. The first CD of that year is J to tha L-O! The Remixes. It gets to number one too. After that, This Is Me... Then gets to number two.</p>

<p>Jennifer's CD Rebirth arrives in 2005. It gets to number two in the U.S. Then Jennifer makes her Spanish CD, Como Ama una Mujer. A lot of Latin people love this CD and it is number one in Mexico.</p>

<p>In September 2007, Jennifer's CD Brave arrives. It doesn't have the same success and only gets to number twelve in the U.S.</p>

<summary>Chapter Six</summary>

<div class="book-page">
  <div class="chapter-label">Chapter Six</div>
  <div class="chapter-title">Success in Business</div>
  <div class="chapter-divider"><span> ~ ~ ~ </span></div>
  <div class="book-body">
    <p class="first-para">Jennifer Lopez is not only an actress. She is not only a dancer and she is not only a singer. She has success in the fashion business too.</p>

<p>This starts in 2001. After the success of The Wedding Planner, Jennifer opens a clothes business with Andy Hilfiger. Andy is the brother of fashion's famous Tommy Hilfiger. They call their business Sweetface. There aren't many clothes for big women but this isn't a problem with Sweetface.</p>

<p>Jennifer starts J.Lo too. From J.Lo, women can buy clothes and perfume too. In a very short time, Jennifer's clothes are famous and her perfume Glow is number one. But some people are angry about Jennifer's business. Some of her clothes come from dead animals, they say. "That's not right." Jennifer listens because she likes animals. Today, those people are happy.</p>

<p>Jennifer is in the food business too. In April 2002, she opens a place in Pasadena, California. Jennifer calls it Madre's. Madre means "mother" in Spanish. The food there is the Cuban food of her mother's family.</p>

<summary>Chapter Seven</summary>

<div class="book-page">
  <div class="chapter-label">Chapter Seven</div>
  <div class="chapter-title">For the Children</div>
  <div class="chapter-divider"><span> ~ ~ ~ </span></div>
  <div class="book-body">
    <p class="first-para">Children are important to Jennifer. She does a lot of charity work for sick children and she helps families with very little money. These are important to Marc too.</p>

<p>Today, Jennifer Lopez and Marc Anthony are famous for their charity work. One night in May 2007, the two stars are in New York with the famous singer Paul Simon. They ask people for money for sick children. The night is a big success. People give a lot of money to the charity.</p>

<p>And now, Jennifer and Marc have children too. Jennifer's friends and family are very happy for them. For Jennifer, children are very important. This time is important too. It is a quiet time. For now, work comes after family.</p>

<p>Jennifer Lopez, the star with many faces. She's a dancer, a singer, an actress, a business woman, and she helps unhappy children and families. Jennifer Lopez does all of these jobs very well.</p>

<p>In the 1990s, Jennifer Lopez was the number one actress and dancer in the U.S. Today, she is famous for many things in many countries. From Asia to America, people ask, "Who is our number one Latina?" The answer is always the same. Jennifer Lopez, of course.</p>

* Где и когда родилась Дженнифер Лопес?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Она родилась 24 июля 1970 года в Бронксе, Нью-Йорк (Bronx, New York).
  </details>

* Какой фильм Дженнифер смотрела сто раз и кто был её кумиром?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Она смотрела фильм «Вестсайдская история» (West Side Story). Её кумиром была Рита Морено (Rita Moreno).
  </details>

* Почему Дженнифер поссорилась с родителями в 1988 году?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Она хотела бросить школу (stop going to school), чтобы стать артисткой (performer). Родители были против.
  </details>

* Какое телешоу помогло Дженнифер переехать в Лос-Анджелес?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Шоу «In Living Color». Она стала танцовщицей в этом шоу.
  </details>

* Какой фильм сделал Дженнифер знаменитой не только в США, но и в латинских странах?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Фильм «Селена» (Selena), где она сыграла мексиканскую певицу Селену Кинтанилью.
  </details>

* В каком фильме 2001 года Дженнифер сыграла свадебную организатора и который стал номером один?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Фильм «Свадебный организатор» (The Wedding Planner).
  </details>

* Как называется первый музыкальный компакт-диск Дженнифер и почему он так называется?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  «On the 6». Название происходит от номера поезда (train number), на котором молодая Дженнифер ездила со станции около своего дома.
  </details>

* В каком году Дженнифер открыла свой бизнес Sweetface и кто ей помогал?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Она открыла Sweetface в 2001 году вместе с Энди Хилфигером (Andy Hilfiger).
  </details>

* Какие имена были у мужей Дженнифер Лопес (по порядку)?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Охани Ноа (Ojani Noa) — 1997, Крис Джадд (Chris Judd) — 2001–2002, Марк Энтони (Marc Anthony) — с 2004 года.
  </details>

* Чем Дженнифер и Марк Энтони занимаются для больных детей и бедных семей?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Они занимаются благотворительностью (charity work), собирают деньги для больных детей и помогают бедным семьям.
  </details>

* Какой компакт-диск Дженнифер 2007 года имел меньший успех?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  «Brave» (сентябрь 2007 года), который занял только 12 место в США.
  </details>
