    <p class="first-para">One day. Friday the 11th. 15:39. I'm writing this vlog in the Double Decker internet cafe in London. The cafe is in a tall old red London bus next to my language school. I love it. It's very British. I'm from Tokyo, but I'm living in the UK for three months. I'm learning English here. My name is Junko. This is me. I've got black hair, dark eyes, glasses and a big smile. Please write to me at my blog. Goodbye for today. Zero messages.</p>

<p>First day. July the 14th. 15:42. Internet, I'm telling you about my life in England because you are my only friend here. I'm living with Mr and Mrs Lacey. Mr Lacey works in a cinema. Mrs Lacey is a policewoman. They always ask me the same thing: "How are you today?" "OK," I answer. I asked about London but Mr Lacey says, "Let's talk this evening. I'm going to work now. Goodbye, Junko. Have a good day." In the evenings I say hello to Mr Lacey, but he says, "I'm very, very tired. A terrible day at the cinema. I'm going to bed now. Good night." Sometimes I feel very lonely. Zero messages.</p>

<p>Tuesday. July the 19th. 15:56. Hello internet. You are not my only friend in England. In the street next to the internet cafe, a girl says to me, "You're Japanese. I know your face from language school. I'm Zsuzsa. I'm from Slovakia. What's your name? I'm Junko. Junko Nagai." Zsuzsa has dark hair and green eyes and expensive jeans. "I love Japan," she says. "Do you know Japan?" I ask. "I know Japanese mobile phones. They're my favourite." We look at my mobile. "That's cool. I want one. Have you got a boyfriend in England? Which boys do you like in school?" "I like Silvio. He's from Sardinia." "I like English boys too. They drive cool cars and they've got great mobile phones. Have you got a car? My father bought me a Fiat Punto, but I want a Mercedes." Zero messages.</p>

<p>Thursday. July the 21st. 15:15. Fifty of us from school are in Oxford. It's an hour from London on the train. Oxford is famous because lots of students live here. Zsuzsa is very happy. "Oxford students have lots of money," she says. But I'm not happy. Zsuzsa isn't talking to me today. She's always with Silvio. Silvio's from Italy. He has short dark hair, exciting eyes and clothes from Sergio Tacchini. His English is very bad. We go to some important buildings in Oxford, but Zsuzsa doesn't look at the buildings. She only looks at Silvio and Silvio only looks at her. They don't want me there with them. I'm writing this in an internet cafe in Oxford. Are Zsuzsa and Silvio in love? What can I say to them on the train to London? Zero messages.</p>

<p>Saturday. July the 23rd. 10:46. It's Saturday morning. Zsuzsa is shopping in Oxford Street with Silvio, not with me. The Laceys are working. Saturday is a big day at the cinema and at the police station. There are only two people in the Double Decker internet cafe: me and the owner. And thirty computers. The owner's name is Jeff. He's old. Maybe twenty-five. He has brown hair and blue eyes. Maybe he reads this blog. He asks me, "Do you like my cafe?" "Very, very much," I say. He smiles and touches my hand. Zero messages.</p>

  <div class="book-body">
    <p class="first-para">Thursday. July the 28th. 15:41. Jeff is watching me a lot today. I look up and he smiles at me. I look back at my computer. I look up again. Again Jeff is looking at me. Does he like me? Maybe we can be friends. Oh, oh, oh! I've got a message on my blog. It's from Slowboy. Thank you, Slowboy. One message.</p>

<p>Thursday. July the 28th. 10:18. Hello Japanese Junko. I like reading your blog. I'm learning Japanese in Tokyo. I'm in Japan for six months. I've got one or two friends here. But they're new friends. Not my old friends back in London. I like Japan, but sometimes I feel lonely too. Write to me. I'm waiting for a message from you. Don't be sad, Slowboy. We can be blog friends. We can laugh about our problems. Zero messages.</p>

<p>Tuesday. August the 2nd. 15:39. In the school cafe, Zsuzsa is looking at Andras's mobile. "That's a cool mobile," she says. He answers, "I'm buying a Suzuki motorbike too. I have the money." Zsuzsa looks into Andras's eyes and smiles. "Do you like my new green T-shirt? It's from Top Shop in Oxford Street." "It's the colour of your eyes, Zsuzsa." "Do you like my jeans?" "Yes, and I like the person in the jeans too." "They're from Gap," she smiles. Where's Silvio? He's looking at Zsuzsa, but Zsuzsa isn't looking at him. Maybe she isn't in love with him today. One message.</p>

<p>Tuesday. August the 2nd. 10:20. There's a girl here in Tokyo. We take the same bus every day. But she's very shy. How can I be friends with her? Be very polite, Slowboy. Open the door of the bus for her. Japanese girls like polite boys. Zero messages.</p>

<p>Friday. August the 5th. 15:44. Silvio is very quiet at school today. He's sad because of Zsuzsa. She sits next to Andras now. Andras's family has lots of money. Andras and Silvio and Zsuzsa never talk to me. But I'm not sad. I've got a blog friend. In the internet cafe there's a hand on my back. I look up. It's Jeff, the owner. I go to a different window on the computer. He can't see my blog now. "Do you want a drink, baby?" "OK." "Let's go to the West End on my motorbike." How can I say no? Jeff is my first friend in England. "Motorbike now?" I say. "Honda. Japanese. Quiet and no problems. Come on." We go down the street very fast. Next to the language school, Zsuzsa and Andras are talking. They see me and smile. Jeff says, "Friends?" "Maybe. I don't know," I answer. "I see. Let's go back. We can say hello to them." "Can I go home now?" "OK, baby. No problem." Zero messages.</p>

<p>Monday. August the 8th. 15:40. "Who's the boy, Junko?" Zsuzsa smiles at me. "He's got a great motorbike." "His name's Jeff." "A Honda," Andras says. "Not bad. But I'm buying a Suzuki 600. It's expensive and fast." "How do you know Jeff?" Zsuzsa asks. "He's a... a friend," I answer. "Oh," Zsuzsa and Andras say. Silvio is listening but he doesn't smile. He's always sad now because of Zsuzsa. He opens the door for me. "Thank you," I say. My face is red. Zsuzsa sits next to me. We are friends again. We talk about Jeff and Andras. "Boys and motorbikes are the same," she says. "Some are exciting. Some are slow." Two messages.</p>

<p>Saturday. August the 6th. 11:21. The girl on the bus is very shy. Her face is always red. She likes you, Slowboy. Ask her to a cafe. Zero messages.</p>

<p>Monday. August the 8th. 18:04. I'm coming back to England soon, Junko. Let's meet in London. OK. Great. Zero messages.</p>

  <div class="chapter-title">Slowboy in London</div>
  <div class="chapter-divider"><span> ~ ~ ~ </span></div>
  <div class="book-body">
    <p class="first-para">Thursday. August the 11th. 15:47. "Hi, Junko." "Hi, Jeff." "Come and talk to me." "First I want to..." "What do you do on the computers?" "I write emails to my family in Japan." "Your family? Cool." Jeff likes touching my hand. He isn't shy. "Come to a party with me, Junko. See you here at eight thirty on Saturday. OK?" How can I say no? It's not polite. "OK," I say. "Great. I love girls with glasses." Zero messages.</p>

<p>Friday. August the 12th. 18:19. "Junko," Silvio says. "I'm going to a cafe with Petra and David from school. Can you come with us?" "Yes, great! Thank you!" It's fun with Silvio and Petra and David. We talk about cinema and sport. I'm bad at sport and Silvio is too. But he's clever and polite. He asks me to the cinema on Saturday night with Petra and David. But I say, "I'm going to a party." He says, "With who?" "You don't know him." "Is it Jeff?" "How do you know about Jeff?" "From you." "Me?" "Oh." "I can go to the cinema and the party." "Say no to the party, Junko. Come with us." How does Silvio know about Jeff? Internet. Zero messages.</p>

<p>Monday. August the 15th. 15:38. On Saturday night Jeff and I go to the party on his motorbike. There are two hundred people in a small room. They are dancing and drinking. "Give me your jacket, Junko. It's hot in here. Let's have a drink." Jeff is dancing. He takes my hand. I dance too. But I don't like dancing. I feel very hot. It's 12:45. Very late. Jeff says, "Give me a kiss, baby. For fun." "I can't. I... I... I don't know the words for..." "A kiss, baby. Not words." He kisses me. I run into the next room. Then I remember. Zsuzsa's number is on my mobile. "Zsuzsa, help me. I'm at a party. I can't get home. I haven't got a car." "I'm in... where are we?" I ask a girl. "Belgravia Drive in Camden." "We are at Belgravia Drive in Camden, Zsuzsa. Come and find me. Please." "OK. I love parties, Junko. See you soon." Today in school Zsuzsa says, "Great party, Junko. I like Jeff. Andras is nice and he's got lots of money. But Jeff is different. He's got no money now, but he's opening a second internet cafe. I've got his mobile number." "Yes. Yes," I say. But I feel sad. I can only go to the internet cafe at lunch time now. Jeff isn't there at lunch. Two messages.</p>

<p>Monday. August the 15th. 15:45. I'm in London. Let's meet tomorrow at the internet cafe. At the internet cafe? I don't know. Zero messages.</p>

<p>Monday. August the 15th. But lunch time is OK. We can meet at one o'clock. OK. At one o'clock. Jeff isn't in the cafe at lunch time. You always remember my problems, Slowboy. Zero messages.</p>

<p>Tuesday. August the 16th. 12:45. In fifteen minutes Slowboy is arriving. Jeff comes into the cafe and sits at his computer. It's lunch time. Why is he here? I put my head down. Please don't see me, Jeff. Bing! A new message on my blog. It's from Slowboy. Four messages.</p>

<p>Tuesday. August the 16th. 12:51. Hello, Junko. I'm here in the cafe. In the cafe? But there are ten boys in the cafe. Which of them is you, Slowboy? Which do you think? Have you got dark hair? You know me, Junko. I don't live in Tokyo. That's only a story. I live in London. A story? I know you. You mean... Yes. That's right. You like computers. I like computers. I look up. Jeff is watching me. Oh no. He smiles and gets up. Slowboy is Jeff. Then one of the boys at the computers gets up too. It's Silvio. He smiles. My face is red. "Hello, Junko," says Silvio. "Hello, Junko," says Jeff. Help me, internet. Which is Slowboy?</p>

## Глава 1 (Right to me)

* Кто такая Юнко (Junko) и почему она находится в Лондоне?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Она из Токио. Она живёт в Великобритании три месяца, чтобы учить английский язык.
  </details>

* Где находится интернет-кафе, в котором Юнко пишет свой блог?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  В старом красном двухэтажном лондонском автобусе (in a tall old red London bus) рядом с её языковой школой.
  </details>

* Как зовут новых друзей Юнко из школы?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Соня (Saunya) из Словакии и Сильвио (Silvio) из Италии.
  </details>

* Как зовут владельца интернет-кафе?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Джефф (Jeff).
  </details>

## Глава 2 (Slow or fast)

* Какое прозвище использует человек, который оставляет сообщения в блоге Юнко?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Slowboy.
  </details>

* Где, по словам Slowboy, он находится и чем занимается?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Он говорит, что находится в Токио и учит японский язык.
  </details>

* Что Юнко советует Slowboy сделать, чтобы подружиться с застенчивой девушкой в автобусе?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Быть очень вежливым и открыть перед ней дверь автобуса (open the door of the bus for her).
  </details>

* Что Джефф предлагает Юнко сделать после того, как кладёт руку ей на спину?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Поехать в Вест-Энд на его мотоцикле (go to the West End on my motorbike).
  </details>

## Глава 3 (Slow boy in London)

* Что говорит Соня о мальчиках и мотоциклах?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  «Одни захватывающие, другие — медленные» (Some are exciting, some are slow).
  </details>

* Куда Сильвио приглашает Юнко в субботу вечером?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  В кино (to the cinema).
  </details>

* Что случилось на вечеринке, когда Джефф попросил Юнко поцеловать его?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Он поцеловал её, а она убежала в другую комнату и позвонила Соне, чтобы та забрала её.
  </details>

* Кто такие Slowboy на самом деле?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  В интернет-кафе встают два человека: Джефф и Сильвио. Юнко не знает, кто из них Slowboy.
  </details>
