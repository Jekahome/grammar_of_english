  <div class="chapter-title">Citizen Barsad</div>
  <div class="chapter-divider"><span> ~ ~ ~ </span></div>
  <div class="book-body">
    <p class="first-para">Charles Darnay stood outside the courtroom. He was free. He held Lucy in his arms.</p>

<p>"He is innocent. I have saved him," said Dr Manette. "Let's go home now."</p>

<p>They returned to their rooms near the bank. Jerry Cruncher and Miss Pross went out to buy some wine. A little later the doctor was talking to Charles and Lucy. Suddenly some revolutionary guards came into the room. One of the guards spoke to Charles.</p>

<p>"Citizen Evrémonde, also called Darnay. You are under arrest," he said.</p>

<p>"Why is he under arrest?" asked Dr Manette. "There was a trial. He is not guilty. He is free now."</p>

<p>"There will be another trial tomorrow," said the guard.</p>

<p>"What has he done wrong?" said Dr Manette angrily. "Who accuses him?"</p>

<p>"You will know tomorrow," said the guard.</p>

<p>The guards took Darnay back to La Force prison. Lucy cried. Her father held her in his arms.</p>

<p>"I have saved him once," said Dr Manette. "I will save him again. I will speak at the trial tomorrow."</p>

<p>Jerry Cruncher and Miss Pross were looking for some wine. They went into Monsieur and Madame Defarge's wine shop. Miss Pross saw a man inside the shop. She cried out, "Solomon!"</p>

<p>"What do you want?" said the man in English. He was angry.</p>

<p>"Solomon," said Miss Pross. "You are my brother, Solomon. I have not seen you for many years."</p>

<p>"Be quiet," said the man. "I have a different name now. I am a revolutionary official. I am an important man in the Republic."</p>

<p>"His name is Citizen Barsad," said another man. They all turned and looked at the person who had spoken.</p>

<p>"Darnay!" said Barsad.</p>

<p>"Why are you here? No. I am not Charles Darnay," said the man. "My name is Sydney Carton. You saw me at the trial in London. My face is like Darnay's face. But I am not Darnay. I want to see Darnay and his wife Lucy Carton. Where are they?"</p>

<p>"Darnay is under arrest," said Barsad. "He is in La Force Prison. He will go to the guillotine."</p>

<div class="book-page">
  <div class="chapter-label">Chapter 11</div>
  <div class="chapter-title">Another Trial</div>
  <div class="chapter-divider"><span> ~ ~ ~ </span></div>
  <div class="book-body">
    <p class="first-para">The next day, Charles Darnay stood in the courtroom again. He stood in front of the judges again. Dr Manette was there. Mr Lorry was there. Lucy was there. Little Lucy was there. Barsad was there. Monsieur and Madame Defarge were there. A crowd of people were standing in the courtroom.</p>

<p>The President of the court shouted, "Charles Saint Evrémonde, also called Charles Darnay. You are an enemy of the Republic. You are an aristocrat. You are a cruel man. Citizen Defarge will speak."</p>

<p>Monsieur Defarge stood up. "I have a paper," he said. "I found this paper in the Bastille prison. It is in Dr Manette's writing. The doctor wrote this paper many years ago. The paper is a story of his life in the Bastille. Please listen."</p>

<p>Monsieur Defarge read the paper. The paper said:</p>

<p>"I am Alexandre Manette. I am a doctor. I am writing this paper in my prison in the Bastille. I am writing it in the year 1767.</p>

<p>One evening in the year 1757, I was walking near the river in Paris. A coach came quickly along the road. It stopped near me. Two men got out of the coach. They took me into the coach. They took me to a house. The house was the home of the Marquis Saint Evrémonde and his brother.</p>

<p>The two brothers showed me a young woman. She was very ill. She had a fever. She was dying. I tried to help her. But she did not get well. She died a few hours later.</p>

<p>The two brothers showed me a young man. He was the woman's brother. He was also very ill. He had a knife in his chest. I tried to help him. He told me this story. 'My sister was going to marry the man she loved. But the Marquis Saint Evrémonde saw her. He wanted her for himself. He took her to his house. He kept her there for a week. He hurt her badly. Then he sent her away. She was very ill. Her lover tried to help her. But the Marquis's brother killed him. My family was poor. We could not do anything. We could not go to the law. The law belongs to the rich. My sister is dying. I am dying. Please help us.' Then the young man died.</p>

<p>The two brothers took me back to my home. But they did not want anybody to know about this story. They wanted to send me away from Paris. I refused to go. I told them I would tell everybody about them. A week later, I was in the Bastille prison. I have been here for ten years. I will die here. I will always hate the Evrémondes. I will always hate the Evrémonde name. I, Alexandre Manette, write this paper. I want everybody to know about the Marquis Saint Evrémonde and his brother. I want everybody to know about this terrible story. I want the Evrémondes to be punished."</p>

<p>When Monsieur Defarge finished reading, everybody in the courtroom looked at Dr Manette. Dr Manette's face was white. He was very ill.</p>

<p>The President spoke to Charles Darnay. "You are the Marquis Saint Evrémonde. You are the son of the younger brother. You are an enemy of the Republic. You are an enemy of the people. Tomorrow you will go to the guillotine."</p>

<summary>Chapter 12</summary>

<div class="book-page">
  <div class="chapter-label">Chapter 12</div>
  <div class="chapter-title">A Plan</div>
  <div class="chapter-divider"><span> ~ ~ ~ </span></div>
  <div class="book-body">
    <p class="first-para">Charles Darnay was in La Force prison. He was waiting for the guillotine. He could not sleep. He was thinking about his wife and his child. He was thinking about Dr Manette and Mr Lorry and Miss Pross. He was thinking about Sydney Carton.</p>

<p>In the morning, a man came to his prison door. The man was wearing a guard's coat. The man opened the door and came into the room.</p>

<p>"Charles Darnay," the man said. "Do you know me?"</p>

<p>Darnay looked at the man. "No," he said. "I do not know you."</p>

<p>"Look at me carefully," the man said. Darnay looked at the man's face. He was surprised. "Sydney Carton!" he said. "How did you get into the prison? Why are you wearing a guard's coat?"</p>

<p>"I have a friend in the prison," Carton said. "His name is Barsad. Barsad is Miss Pross's brother. He helped me. He gave me this coat. He opened the door for me. Now listen to me. I have a plan. You will get out of the prison. You will go to England. You will go home to Lucy and your child."</p>

<p>"How can I get out of the prison?" asked Darnay.</p>

<p>"You will change clothes with me," said Carton. "You will wear my clothes. I will wear your clothes. I will stay here. You will go out. Barsad will help you. He will take you to Mr Lorry. Mr Lorry will take you to Lucy. You will all go to England tonight."</p>

<p>"No," said Darnay. "I cannot do that. You will die. They will kill you."</p>

<p>"They will not kill me," said Carton. "They will not know about the plan. Do not worry about me. I want to do this. I love Lucy. I want to help her. I want to help you. I want to help your child. Please do this for me."</p>

<p>Darnay looked at Carton. He saw that Carton was serious. He saw that Carton would not change his mind.</p>

<p>"All right," Darnay said. "I will do it."</p>

<p>They changed clothes. Darnay put on Carton's clothes. Carton put on Darnay's clothes. Carton sat down on the bed. He put his head in his hands.</p>

<p>"Go now," he said. "Go quickly."</p>

<p>Barsad came to the door. He looked at the man on the bed. He thought the man was Darnay. He did not look carefully. He took Darnay out of the prison.</p>

<summary>Chapter 13</summary>

<div class="book-page">
  <div class="chapter-label">Chapter 13</div>
  <div class="chapter-title">The Guillotine</div>
  <div class="chapter-divider"><span> ~ ~ ~ </span></div>
  <div class="book-body">
    <p class="first-para">Barsad took Darnay to Mr Lorry's rooms. Mr Lorry was waiting there. Lucy and her child were there too. Dr Manette was there. Miss Pross was there.</p>

<p>"Charles!" Lucy cried. "You are free! How did you get out of the prison?"</p>

<p>"Sydney Carton helped me," Darnay said. "He is in the prison now. He is wearing my clothes. He will die for me."</p>

<p>Lucy's face went white. "No!" she cried. "Not Sydney! He cannot die for you! We must go back! We must save him!"</p>

<p>"We cannot go back," Mr Lorry said. "The guards will catch us. We must go to England now. We must go tonight. We will take the coach to Calais. We will take the ship to Dover. We will be safe in England. We will go now."</p>

<p>They went to the coach. They got into the coach. The coach went quickly through the streets of Paris. They left Paris. They were going to Calais. They were going to England.</p>

<p>In the prison, Carton was waiting. He was sitting on the bed. He was thinking about Lucy. He was thinking about Charles. He was thinking about their child. He was thinking about his life. He was not afraid. He was ready to die.</p>

<p>In the morning, the guards came to the prison. They took Carton to the guillotine. A crowd of people were standing around the guillotine. Madame Defarge was in the crowd. She was watching. She was knitting. She was counting the heads.</p>

<p>Carton stood in front of the guillotine. He looked at the crowd. He saw Madame Defarge. He saw her face. He saw her eyes. He was not afraid. He remembered the words of Jesus Christ: "I am the resurrection and the life. Whoever believes in me will live, even if he dies."</p>

<p>Carton spoke to the crowd. He said, "I am doing this for a friend. I am doing this for love. I am doing this for a better life. I am doing this for a better world. I am not afraid. I am ready."</p>

<p>The blade of the guillotine fell. Sydney Carton was dead.</p>

<p>In England, Charles and Lucy Darnay lived a long and happy life. They never forgot Sydney Carton. They told their child about him. They told their grandchildren about him. They told everybody about him. They told them about his brave and noble sacrifice. They told them about his great love.</p>

## Глава 1 (Дувр)

* Кто такой Джерри Кранчер (Jerry Cruncher) и какое сообщение он привёз мистеру Лорри (Mr. Lorry)?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Джерри Кранчер — посыльный из банка Телсона. Он привёз письмо, в котором говорилось, что доктор Манетт (Dr. Manette) жив и находится в Париже, в винном магазине Дефаржей.
  </details>

* Что случилось с доктором Манеттом 18 лет назад?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Он был заключён в тюрьму Бастилия (Bastille) на 18 лет без суда.
  </details>

## Глава 2 (Винный магазин в Париже)

* Чем занимался доктор Манетт в маленькой комнате, когда его нашли мистер Лорри и Люси?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Он сидел и шил обувь (making shoes).
  </details>

* Как доктор Манетт представился, когда мистер Лорри спросил его имя?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Он сказал: «Заключённый номер 105, Северная башня» (Prisoner number 105, North Tower).
  </details>

* Кто помогал Люси и мистеру Лорри ухаживать за доктором Манеттом на корабле по пути в Англию?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Молодой человек по имени Чарльз Дарней (Charles Darnay).
  </details>

## Глава 3 (Суд Олд-Бейли)

* В чём обвиняли Чарльза Дарнея на суде в Лондоне?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Его обвиняли в шпионаже (spy) в пользу Франции.
  </details>

* Кто помог оправдать Дарнея, и каким образом?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Адвокат Страйвер (Stryver) показал суду своего клерка Сидни Картона (Sidney Carton), который был очень похож на Дарнея, доказав, что свидетель мог перепутать обвиняемого.
  </details>

## Глава 4 (Новые друзья)

* Чем Чарльз Дарней и Сидни Картон были похожи внешне?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  У них были одинаковые лица (their faces were the same).
  </details>

* О чём Сидни Картон сказал Дарнею после суда, выпив много вина?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Он сказал, что хочет выпить и думать о Люси Манетт, потому что она очень красива.
  </details>

## Глава 5 (Аристократ)

* Что случилось с ребёнком, когда карета маркиза Сент-Эвремона (Marquis St. Evrémonde) ехала по улицам Парижа?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Лошади переехали ребёнка, и он умер.
  </details>

* Как отреагировал маркиз на смерть ребёнка?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Он бросил золотую монету и сказал, что это не его проблема.
  </details>

* Кто убил маркиза Сент-Эвремона и как?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Гаспар (Gaspar), отец погибшего ребёнка. Он заколол маркиза ножом (knife) в его постели.
  </details>

## Глава 6 (Свадьба)

* За кого вышла замуж Люси Манетт?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  За Чарльза Дарнея.
  </details>

* Какое имя (титул) Чарльз Дарней скрывал, живя в Англии?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Он был маркизом Сент-Эвремоном (Marquis St. Evrémonde).
  </details>

## Глава 7 (Революция)

* Какое событие произошло в Париже в июле 1789 года?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Народ взял штурмом Бастилию (the Bastille prison), началась революция.
  </details>

* Кто прислал Дарнею письмо с просьбой о помощи?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Габель (Gabelle), старый слуга семьи Сент-Эвремон.
  </details>

## Глава 8 (В Париж)

* Что случилось с Дарнеем, когда он приехал во Францию?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Его арестовали как аристократа и врага республики.
  </details>

* В какую тюрьму посадили Дарнея?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  В тюрьму Ла-Форс (La Force prison).
  </details>

## Глава 9 (Враг Республики)

* Кто приехал из Лондона в Париж, чтобы спасти Дарнея?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Доктор Манетт, Люси с дочерью, мисс Прос (Miss Pross) и Джерри Кранчер.
  </details>

* Почему доктор Манетт верил, что сможет помочь Дарнею на суде?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Он был знаменитым бывшим узником Бастилии, и революционеры его уважали.
  </details>

* Какое решение вынес суд после первого слушания дела Дарнея?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  «Не виновен» (Not guilty), Дарней был освобождён.
  </details>

## Глава 10 (Гражданин Барсад)

* Что случилось с Дарнеем сразу после того, как он вышел из зала суда?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Его снова арестовали по новому обвинению.
  </details>

* Кто такой Соломон / гражданин Барсад (Citizen Barsad)?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Это брат мисс Прос, работающий революционным чиновником.
  </details>

* Кто пришёл в винный магазин Дефаржей и сказал, что хочет увидеть Дарнея и Люси?
  <details> 
  <summary>ответ:</summary>
  Сидни Картон (Sydney Carton).
  </details>
