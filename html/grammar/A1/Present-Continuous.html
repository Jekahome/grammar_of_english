The Present Continuous Tense

Действие происходит в данный момент или о запланированном действии в будущем.

Слова маркеры:

  • at the moment
  • now
  • today
  • tonight
  • this week/year

to be:

  • I - am
  • He/She/It - is
  • You/We/They - are

Утверждение

Актив:

Subject + to be + V+ing

  • She is playing tennis now - Она сейчас играет в теннис.
  • I am reading a novel at the moment - Я в данный момент читаю новелу.
  • What are you doing now? - I am sunbathing. And you? I am playing tennis. - Что ты сейчас делаешь? Я загораю. А ты? Я играю в теннис.
  • My mother is cooking dinner at the moment. - Моя мама сейчас готовит обед.
  • I am playing chess with Bill this Sunday. - Я играю с Биллом в шахматы в это воскресенье. (запланированное действие)
  • My father is not working today. - Мой отец сегодня не работает.
  • Usually I finish work at five, but this week I am working till 7 p.m. - Обычно я заканчиваю работу в 5, но на этой неделе я работаю до 7.
  • Tim is a builder. But this summer he is working as a clown. - Тим строитель. Но этим летом он работает клоуном. (запланированное действие)

Пассив:

Subject + to be + V3

  • These patients are being treated more effectively - Этих пациентов лечат более эффективно.

Вопрос

Актив:

to be + Subject + V+ing

  • Is she cooking at the moment? - Она готовит в данный момент?

Пассив:

to be + Subject + being + V3

  • Are these patients being treated more effectively? - Этих пациентов лечат более эффективно?

Отрицание

Актив:

Subject + to be not + V+ing

  • She isn't cooking at the moment - В данный момент она не готовит.

Пассив:

Subject + to be not + being + V3

  • These patients are not being treated more effectively - Этих пациентов не лечат более эффективно.

Упражнение

  1. Дополнить речь:

    2. The tall one (talk) to somebody on the phone. The short one (smoke). What ... the fat one (do)? ... he (smoke), too? No, he (smoke). He (watch) TV. What programme ... he (watch)? I am not sure. A talk show, I guess. Oh, the tall one is in the kitchen now. He (cook) something. What ... he (cook)? He (fry) eggs. And the short one (drink) Cola. ... they (do) anything suspicious? No, they aren't. Everything is very quiet here.

  2. Поставить глаголы в правильную форму:
    • In summer it (rain) a lot in the tropics. But right now it (not rain) and the sun (shine) brightly.
    • My mother is a singer. She (sing) a beautiful song. She (give) a concert right now.
    • My sister Lulu always (wear) trousers. But today is a special day - it's her birthday and she (wear) a fantastic dress.
    • My dad is usually very busy, but this week he (not work) and we (spend) a lot of time together.
    • Usually people (start) leaving these places early in September. But this year is different. It is October, and they (stay).
    • It (get) dark early in winter. It's 4 p.m. only and it (get) dark now.
    • I (not eat) much in the mornings.
  3. Составить утверждение, вопрос, отрицание:
    • I, remember, his address - I remember his address.
    • you, like, your new teacher? - Do you like your new teacher?
    • you, need, my help - You don't need my help.
    • What, this, word, mean? - What does this word mean?
    • Polly, want, to see the doctor - Polly wants to see the doctor.
    • I, hate, this hot weather - I hate this hot weather.
    • My friends, cheat, at the moment - My friends are cheating at the moment.
    • In, Norway, it, snow, hard, in winter - In Norway, it snows hard in winter.
    • Ellie, wear, a beautiful blouse, today - Ellie is wearing a beautiful blouse today.
    • My mom, wear, glasses - My mom wears glasses.
    • Jack's, teacher, be, very kind - Jack's teacher is very kind.
    • You, have breakfast, now? - Are you having breakfast now?
